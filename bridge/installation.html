Installation — BerelzBridge Free

BerelzBridge Free is available here for download. Pro is purchased through MQL5 Market.

1
Download BerelzBridgeFree.ex5 BerelzBridgeFree.ex5  FREE
2
Copy to MT5 Copy BerelzBridgeFree.ex5 into your MT5 Data Folder / MQL5 / Indicators
3
Refresh Navigator In MT5: right-click Navigator → Refresh
4
Drag onto chart Find BerelzBridgeFree under Indicators → drag onto any chart → click OK
5
Find your JSON file MQL5/Files/ folder — filename: symbol_tf.json (e.g. xaueur_m5.json)
Installation — BerelzBridge Pro
1
Purchase on MQL5 Market mql5.com/en/market/product/165398
2
Download from MQL5 account After purchase, download from My Products in the MT5 Market tab
3
Drag onto chart Find in Navigator → Market → drag onto any chart → configure settings → OK
4
Find your JSON file MQL5/Files/ folder — filename: symbol_stream.json (e.g. xaueur_stream.json)
